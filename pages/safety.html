Discord is a place dedicated to talking and hanging out with your friends, family, and communities. Millions of diverse servers live on Discord, from small groups of your closest friends to huge communities where thousands connect over shared interests.

We're committed to making sure that Discord is a welcoming place for all. Learn how to shape the best experience for you and find the resources you need, whether you're a teen, parent, educator, or just looking to stay safe.
